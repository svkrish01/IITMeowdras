Common Misconceptions

"Milk is good for cats."

Most cats are actually lactose intolerant. Giving them cow's milk can cause digestive upset and diarrhea. Stick to fresh water!

"Cats always land on their feet."

While they have a "righting reflex," it doesn't always work, especially from short falls. They can and do get seriously injured from falls.

"Indoor cats can't get sick."

They are much safer, but they can still get airborne germs, parasites from houseflies, or illnesses from vet visits. Regular vaccinations are still important.

Cat-Friendly Foods

Safe Foods (Treats)

  • Cooked Chicken (no skin/bones)
  • Cooked Fish (no bones)
  • Cooked Eggs
  • Small amounts of plain yogurt
  • Catnip or Cat Grass

TOXIC Foods (Avoid!)

  • Onions & Garlic
  • Chocolate
  • Grapes & Raisins
  • Alcohol & Caffeine
  • Milk (most cats are lactose intolerant)
